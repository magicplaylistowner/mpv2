Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

How do promoted songs work?

As soon as you submit a song for promotion, our team will review it and determine if we are good fit. You will receive a confirmation email with a link to complete your payment within 24 hours.

Once you’re done, your song will start showing up in genre specific playlists. Upon reaching your campaign goal, you will receive a detailed report on how the promotion impacted your Spotify profile.

While we are in Beta, your song will also be displayed on the homescreen under the Independent Artists section.

Is this for me?

Yes, if you want real users to listen to your song. We focus on independent artists who are just starting out. This would be in the range of 0 to 1000 monthly listeners. However, If you don't qualify, send us your song anyway, we may still be able to help :).

What are the available plans?

Currently we have 3 plans:

  • 1000 playlists.
  • 2500 playlists.
  • 500 playlists.

While we are in Beta, your song will also be displayed on the homescreen under the Independent Artists section.

Do we work with playlists curators?

No. We don’t work with curators. Your song will be showcased to our users when they come to MagicPlaylist to discover new music.
